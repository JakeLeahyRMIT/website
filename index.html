Julie Porteous

I'm an Associate Professor in the School of Computing Technologies at RMIT University, Melbourne. In the past I've had lecturing posts at Teeside University, University of Durham and The Open University along with post-docs at University of Huddersfield and University of Strathclyde.

Research Interests

  • Automated Planning
  • Interactive Narrative (application of automated planning to narrative generation)
  • Automated acquisition of AI planning domain models
  • Authoring tools for Interactive Narrative
